Privacy Policy

YAMU Account Management Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Process

This account management portal processes the following information:

  • Authentication Data: Google account information used for sign-in
  • Account Information: Your YAMU profile data including name, email, and preferences
  • Trip Data: Travel plans, locations, and trip details you've created or participated in
  • Social Data: Friend connections and invitation history
  • Usage Data: Achievements, comments, and app usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

This portal uses your information solely for:

  • Account Verification: Confirming your identity for account management actions
  • Data Export: Providing you with a complete copy of your data
  • Account Deletion: Permanently removing your data from YAMU services

3. Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • Secure Authentication: Google OAuth2 for verified access
  • Encrypted Transmission: All data transfer uses HTTPS encryption
  • No Data Storage: This portal does not store any of your data locally
  • Direct Firebase Connection: Your data is accessed directly from secure Firebase servers

4. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View and export all your YAMU data
  • Data Portability: Download your data in a machine-readable format
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data through the main YAMU app
  • Erasure: Request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using YAMU services at any time

5. Account Deletion Process

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and authentication data are permanently removed
  • Your trips are either transferred to other participants or deleted
  • Friend connections and invitations are removed
  • All comments, achievements, and statistics are deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

6. Data Retention

Once you delete your account:

  • All personal data is immediately marked for deletion
  • Data is permanently removed from active systems within 30 days
  • Backup systems are purged within 90 days
  • No personal data is retained after this period

7. Third-Party Services

This portal uses:

  • Google Authentication: For secure sign-in (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase: For data access (subject to Google Cloud Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting this portal (subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy)

8. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@yamu.app
  • Support: support@yamu.app
  • Website: yamu.app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Continued use of this portal after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You explicitly request account management actions
  • Legitimate Interest: Providing account management services you've requested
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with data protection regulations
